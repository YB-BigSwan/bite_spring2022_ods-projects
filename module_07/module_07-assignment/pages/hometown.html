North Ogden

I grew up in a small mountain-side town called North Ogden, UT. It's a quiet place where not much happens and everyone knows each other. This is mostly because no one ever really leaves. It's a comfortable place and if you like to spend your time in the mountains it's a great place to be.

Like all places, it comes with it's ups and downs. Living in such a tight knit community can feel a bit suffocating at times because everyone sees everything you do. It's also not very diverse and is heavily religious. So people who are different can find themselves feeling like they're on the outside.

However, if you can get past that, you'll be spoiled with mountains just outside your back door, a beautiful lake 15 minutes away, and 3 world-class ski resorts that you can drive to in under 30 minutes.
