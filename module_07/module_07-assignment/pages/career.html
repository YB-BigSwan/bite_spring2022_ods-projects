Future Career Plans

Previously, I had planned to continue studying to get my Master's in psychology, in hopes of one day opening my own practice. However, after a 2 year internship at a residential treatment center, I discovered that it wasn't the right career for me. As much as I love helping others, the job was too emotionally draining for me and by the end of those two years, I dreaded going into the office. So I knew I had to find a new path.

After the internship was over I took a year off to figure myself out. After asking myself what I wanted from my career, I decided that I wanted to find a career where I could solve problems, be creative, and have a secure job. A few weeks later, I was talking to a friend about this. He is a web developer, and he thought that I might enjoy the same sort of work. So, he pointed me towards a few online classes to try things out before fully committing and after the first few lessons I was hooked.

That brings us to now. I'm currently studying IT(CS) at Haaga-Helia University of Applied Sciences in order to gain the skills necessary to start my career as a web or mobile developer.
